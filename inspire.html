John Elway

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Hard-working and determined. Elway was always true to his code.
  • Iconic. John Elway is known for being one of the greatest players of all time.
  • Role model. Elway won the Walter Payton award in 1992 proving some characteristic traits off the field.
  • Loyal. Elway spent his entire career with the Broncos and is now apart of the front office.

Over the course of my lifetime no single football player has impacted me as much as John Elway has. I have always admired his play and his passion off the field. Elway is recognized as the greatest Bronco player of all time and provides a great example to all players in the NFL. There is never going to be another person like him as he has a legendary status.
